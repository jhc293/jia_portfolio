Welcome to my anime page! Over here, you’ll be able to see a comprehensive list of my top nine favorite anime of all time! Anime are japanese illustrations of animation typically voiced in Japanese, but can also be found dubbed in local languages or be in other languages subtitles with the original japanese voices. For the best experience, it is best to watch anime in its original japanese voices with your local language subtitles. I hope that after reading this list, you give one, if not all of the anime a try!

Jia's Top Anime Picks

Kill La Kill

This is one of my favorite anime of all time. Kotaku calls it “Kill la Kill is a rare breed of anime. It perfectly mixes comedy and action while reveling in taking everything to the extreme. And the fact that it has now ended leaves each week looking a little less bright.” From the fight scenes, to the hyperactive and fast paced plot, you’ll find something you’ll love in this show! Fight scenes: 10/10 Plot: 11/10 Overall Rating: 1000/10

One Piece

Infamously known for one of the longest running mainstream anime, One piece has a lovely and heartwarming story as the Strawhat pirates lead by Monkey D. Luffy set sail to find the One Piece treasure. The characters have great development, tears will be shed, and tears of joy will also be shed. The action in this show is incredible and has strong comedic elements. No wonder this anime has aired for over 20 years since its debut in 1999.

My Hero Academia

In a world everyone has random superpowers, called quirks *really just 80% of the world population, tho almost everyone in japan has one. However, a boy named izuku midoriya, who was born quirkless, gets chosen by the world’s greatest hero, all might, to inherit his quirk! He attends ua high, the most prestigious superhero school in the world, and trains to be a hero! Fight scenes in this show are 10/10 and plot is a 9/10 Overall Rating: 9/10

The God of High School

This show is technically not an anime but an animated manhwa meaning that it has korean animation origins. However, for our purposes they are the same. The God of High school has the best fight scenes out of all anime I’ve watched here. In this tournament style anime follow the trio: Yu-Mira, Hon- DaeWi, and Jin Mori; as they embark on their quest to see who will reign supreme in the God of High School Tournament. As far as plot goes for this story, it is a little confusing for a short series (13 episodes) but enjoyable nontheless! Plot: 8/10 Fights: 11/10 Overall Rating: 9.625/10

Mob-Psycho 100

Eighth-grader Shigeo "Mob" Kageyama has tapped into his inner wellspring of psychic prowess at a young age. But the power quickly proves to be a liability when he realizes the potential danger in his skills. Choosing to suppress his power, Mob's only present use for his ability is to impress his longtime crush, Tsubomi, who soon grows bored of the same tricks. In order to effectuate control on his skills, Mob enlists himself under the wing of Arataka Reigen, a con artist claiming to be a psychic, who exploits Mob's powers for pocket change. Now, exorcising evil spirits on command has become a part of Mob's daily, monotonous life. (My Animelist) Plot: 10/10 Fights: 10/10 Overall Rating: 10/10

Hunter X Hunter:

In this beloved series, follow Gon, Killua, Kurapika, and Leorio as they go through trials and tribulations to become the next ‘hunter’. A licensure examination that has strict physical and mental burdens. The fights and plot in this show are extremely well created and highly well articulated without being too convoluted. You will enjoy every arc especially the chimera ant arc. Story: 11/10 Plot: 10/10 Overall Rating: 11/10

Attack on Titan

It is set in a world where humanity lives within cities surrounded by enormous walls that protect them from gigantic man-eating humanoids referred to as Titans; the story follows Eren Yeager, who vows to retake the world after a Titan brings about the destruction of his hometown and the death of his mother. This show will bring tingles down your spine. This action packed, thriller-esque anime has a huge fan base. After giving this soon to be 5 season anime a watch, you would probably be part of that same fanbase. Plot: 12/10 Fight scenes: 11/10 Overall Rating: 11.99/10

Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood

Set in a fictional world where alchemy reigns, the elric brothers are off to seek the philosopher’s stone. Due to an alchemy experiment that killed his brother and mother, Edward Elric wanted to seek the stone’s power to revive his brother Alphonse's body who is now contained in a metal armor. As they find out more clues about the stone, there are others who already possess the power of the philosopher’s stone and want to wreak havoc. It’s up to the Elric brothers to save the day. Plot: 10/10 Fights: 10/10

Bleach

No it’s not Sodium-Hypochlorite. This anime is about Ichigo Kurosaki who accidentally absorbs a soul reaper Rukia’s soul reaper powers to become a soul reaper himself. After finding the secret to the soul society and bringing his friends to save Rukia . Ichigo and his friends set out to the soul society to find Rukia. After fighting the members of the soul society, it turns out that some of the soul reapers have their mission to fight against those in soul society. Bleach is one of the big three anime, along with One Piece and Naruto (not listed) Plot: 8/10 Fights: 10/10 Overall Rating: 9/10

Noragami

In times of need, if you look in the right place, you just may see a strange telephone number scrawled in red. If you call this number, you will hear a young man introduce himself as the Yato God. Yato is a minor deity and a self-proclaimed "Delivery God," who dreams of having millions of worshippers. Without a single shrine dedicated to his name, however, his goals are far from being realized. He spends his days doing odd jobs for five yen apiece, until his weapon partner becomes fed up with her useless master and deserts him. Just as things seem to be looking grim for the god, his fortune changes when a middle school girl, Hiyori Iki, supposedly saves Yato from a car accident, taking the hit for him. Plot: 9/10 Fights: 9.5/10 Overall Rating: 9.5/10
